SongBridge is your new central music management tool.

Manage all of your music database in one single place. SongBridge allows you to easily create, organize, share, and synchronize your music library on any service that you use.

Organize

SongBridge is a smart blend of best features that you can find on public music services. It offers library management tools that are best-suited for smart playlist creation, archiving, and enables you to have all of your libray in one place.
Don't forget your good ol' song that you don't listen for now !

Synchronize

Import library from any platform, export to an other, and automatically synchronize playlists between them. You can switch between multiple music streaming services, import old iTunes playlists, or even go back to offline listening. SongBridge is platform-agnostic !

Dynamize

SongBridge allows you to create dynamic playlists that get updated everytime a new song from artists that you pick is released.

Get the same music everywhere

Either you listen music through streaming services, watching video clips, or with offline mp3's, you get the same playlists. If you add song to one of them, it appears on every platform.

Centralize your music library

Every songs are not available on each platform. It could be frustrating to have differents playlists on differents music services. Now you can have one central place to manage your library, and you can know which song of your playlist are availables on which platform.

SongBridge main goal is not to play music, but instead focused on managing your library. However it is possible to listen to music from platform that allows it such as SoundCloud.
